Chicken Soup

You'll want to put a ring on this recipe.

This one-pot meal couldn't be easier to make. It takes about an hour, though a large portion of that is hands-off cooking on the stovetop.

Ingridients:

  • Sun-dried tomatoes
  • Aromatics
  • Broth
  • Pasta
  • Chicken
  • Seasoning and garnishes

How to make chicken soup step by step:

  1. Gather all ingridients
  2. Heat tomato oil with onion and garlic
  3. Add tomato paste and sun-dried tomatoes
  4. Stir in everything else
  5. Add pasta and continue cooking
  6. Finish off with chicken, spinach and cheese
  7. Garnish with additional parmesan and serve

Enjoy!
